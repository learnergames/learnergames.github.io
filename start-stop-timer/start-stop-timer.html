Interval Timer: Stay on Track

Get more done and stay focused with this simple, easy-to-use interval timer. Whether you are hitting the gym for a workout or sitting down for a deep focus session, this app helps you manage your time without any complicated setup.

Why you’ll love it:

  • Simple & Fast: Set your "Start" and "Stop" times in seconds and get moving.

  • Clear Progress: Easy-to-read tiles show exactly which step you are on and how much time is left.

  • Remembers Your Spot: If you close the app or get a phone call, your timer stays right where you left it.

  • Easy on the Eyes: Switch between Light and Dark mode whenever you want.

  • No Fluff: No confusing menus or technical jargon—just a clean timer that works.

Perfect for:

  • HIIT and Gym Workouts

  • Circuit Training

  • Study Sprints (Pomodoro)

  • Cooking and Chores

Stay in the zone and keep your momentum going. Download Interval Timer and take the guesswork out of your routine!
